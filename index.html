OUR STORY

The Underground
Rises Above

In the heart of Al Mankhool, where Dubai's golden skyline meets the mystique of the Rooftop soul, UPYARD was born. A sanctuary for those who seek more than just a night out — a full sensory journey draped in crimson, gold, and smoke.

From our Pioneer DJ consoles pulsing with energy, to live belly dance and cabaret performances straight from the theatre of life — every night at UPYARD is a production unlike any other in Dubai.
